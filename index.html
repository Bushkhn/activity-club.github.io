ENRICHING CHILDREN’S EDUCATION THROUGH THE PERFORMING ARTS

Activity and rest are two vital aspects of life. To find a balance in them is a skill in itself. Wisdom is knowing when to have rest, when to have activity, and how much of each to have. Finding them in each other - activity in rest and rest in activity - is the ultimate freedom.

We love creativity at Let's Grow Together – and it goes without saying that we’re mad about drama!

Our range of performing arts-based sessions for nurseries, preschool settings and primary schools give young children a strong foundation in their early years’ education. They also spark their natural curiosity, nurture their imaginations and help bridge performance gaps. All of our clubs, classes and workshops are delivered by hand-picked performers who use their talents as singers, dancers, and actors to make learning fun. They encourage children to unleash their imaginations by expressing themselves creatively and what’s more, they get children active!

What we do

Fun Times Activity Club Ltd was set up in June 2021. Our aim is to provide a bespoke breakfast and after school club to ensure children are in a safe, stimulating and happy environment.

School Workshops

Our school workshops and clubs are for children aged 5 – 11 years, and we tailor each one to your school’s needs.Whatever subject you’re teaching right now, we can deliver a workshop that will help enrich your students’ learning!
